Privacy Policy

隐私政策

Last updated: June 24, 2026

最后更新：2026 年 6 月 24 日

1. Information We Collect

1. 我们收集的信息

License Verification: When you use a Pro license key, the plugin sends your license key and domain name to our license verification server. This is necessary to validate your license.

License 验证：当您使用专业版 License Key 时，插件会将您的 License Key 和域名发送到我们的 License 验证服务器。这是验证您的授权的必要步骤。

Payment Processing: Payments are handled entirely by Paddle. We do not collect, store, or process any payment information (credit card numbers, billing addresses). Please refer to Paddle's privacy policy for how they handle your data.

付款处理：付款完全由 Paddle 处理。我们不收集、存储或处理任何付款信息（信用卡号、账单地址）。请参阅 Paddle 的隐私政策了解他们如何处理您的数据。

2. How We Use Your Information

2. 我们如何使用您的信息

Your email address (provided during purchase) is used only to deliver your license key and communicate about license-related issues. We do not send marketing emails.

您的邮箱地址（在购买时提供）仅用于发送 License Key 和沟通授权相关问题。我们不会发送营销邮件。

3. Data Sharing

3. 数据共享

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personal information to third parties, except as required by law.

我们不出售、交易或向第三方转让您的个人信息，除非法律要求。

4. Data Retention

4. 数据保留

License validation logs may be retained for up to 90 days. Your purchase records are retained as required by tax regulations.

License 验证日志最多保留 90 天。您的购买记录按照税务法规要求保留。

5. Your Rights

5. 您的权利

You may request access to, correction, or deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting us.

您可以随时通过联系我们请求访问、更正或删除您的个人数据。

6. Contact

6. 联系我们

If you have questions about this policy: Contact us.

如果您对本政策有疑问：联系我们。
